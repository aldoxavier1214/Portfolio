E-commerce website

Library Book Management

A library management system using CRUD allows users to Create, Read, Update, and Delete book records, manage user accounts, track book loans and returns, and maintain inventory efficiently. It streamlines operations, enhances user experience, and ensures organized book management in libraries.

Learning Management System

A Learning Management System (LMS) is a digital platform that facilitates online education by managing, delivering, and tracking courses and learning materials, enabling educators to create content and students to access and engage with it anytime, anywhere.

Student Mark Generator

A student mark generator is a tool that automatically generates and manages student grades, calculates averages, and may provide insights into performance. It simplifies record-keeping and report generation, ensuring accurate and efficient tracking of academic achievements.

Personal Dashboard

A personal dashboard provides a centralized view of key metrics, tasks, and data, enabling users to monitor and manage their activities efficiently. It often includes customizable widgets for tracking progress, goals, and important updates in a single, intuitive interface.
